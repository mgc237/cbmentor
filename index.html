Welcome to CB Mentoring

Supporting young Black and minority ethnic youth in and around Cambridge

DONATE   CHECK OUR PROGRAM

Sign your child up to our Saturday School

We are now in the second year of our popular Saturday School. The scheme was one of the main reasons CB Mentoring was created, as many of the founding members had fond memories of the role Saturday School played in their own educational and cultural educations as teens. The programme allows students to be paired up with university of Cambridge students who act as tutors whilst our young people conduct their homework
